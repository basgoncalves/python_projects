This is a list of Billboard magazine's Top Hot 100 songs of 2001.[1]

"Hanging by a Moment" by Lifehouse (pictured) was the best-performing single of 2001.
Destiny's Child placed three songs on the list. The highest was their 11-week number-one song "Independent Women", ranking at number 10.
